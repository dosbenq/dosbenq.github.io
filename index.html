My career started on the engineering side, building navigation and ADAS systems that went into cars from BMW, Audi, MAN, and Daimler. Duke's MEM program (3.92 GPA) has been the bridge to the business side. I am studying product strategy, financial analysis, and technology management while building PointsMax, an AI-powered rewards platform, from scratch. The goal is simple: become the kind of PM who can talk to engineers and executives with equal fluency.
